---> static: Default positioning, no effect from top, right, bottom, left.
---> relative: Positioned relative to its normal position, affecting only itself.
---> absolute: Positioned relative to its nearest positioned ancestor or initial containing block, removed from normal flow.
---> fixed: Positioned relative to the viewport, always in the same place even when scrolling.
---> sticky: Positioned based on the scroll position, toggling between relative and fixed.
